Legacy Bearer

A legacy gracefully carried forward.

Jainesh Seth represents the new generation of leadership at Shri Murari Jewellers Pvt. Ltd. — a name synonymous with purity, craftsmanship and unwavering trust. With a refined eye for detail and a deep respect for tradition, he embodies the bridge between timeless heritage and contemporary luxury.

His vision is rooted in preserving the values that built the family name while embracing modern retail experiences that elevate the way connoisseurs engage with fine jewellery. Every decision reflects a commitment to authenticity, transparency and an uncompromising standard of excellence.

For Jainesh, jewellery is not merely an ornament — it is a celebration of milestones, relationships and the enduring beauty of Indian artistry.
